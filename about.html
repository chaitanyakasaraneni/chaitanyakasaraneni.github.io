I'm a Software Engineer - Data at Egen Solutions with over 4 years of experience building cloud-based data solutions and ETL pipelines. I specialize in transforming complex data challenges into practical, scalable applications that drive real-world impact.

With a Master's degree in Computer Engineering from San Jose State University and a strong background in data engineering, machine learning, and cloud technologies, I architect robust systems that process vast amounts of structured and unstructured data. My expertise includes designing ETL workflows, building data infrastructure on cloud platforms, and collaborating with scientific and medical experts to develop innovative solutions.

Beyond technical skills, I'm passionate about continuous learning and knowledge sharing. I actively contribute to the tech community through writing, open-source projects, and research. I thrive in challenging environments where I can solve complex problems, mentor team members, and drive technological innovation to create meaningful solutions.
